We are Arctic Monkeys

Arctic Monkeys was originally formed in 2002 by four friends. Alex Turner, Matt Helders, Jamie Cook, and Andy Nicholson. Alex, Jamie and Matt were neighbours and close friends, and they met Andy in secondary school and played together in their teens.

They were later inspired by the local music scene and began performing together in pubs. They soon gained popularity through word of mouth and the rise of social media. This allowed them to build a fanbase before signing with a record label and releasing their first album.

Alex Turner

Alex Turner

Alex Turner is the main vocalist and one of two guitarists in the band Arctic Monkeys. He is also one of the founding members and has been a part of the band since 2002 when Alex was just 15. He is currently 39 years old and still in the band even though they haven’t released music since 2022.

Matt Helders

Matt Helders

Matt Helders is one of the founding members of the band and has been their drummer since 2002 when he was also 15 years old. Matt is also currently 39 years old and still a part of the band.

Andy Nicholson

Andy Nicholson

Andy Nicholson was in the band originally as their bassist when it formed in 2002 when he was 15. He then wanted to take a break from touring in 2006 from exhaustion. This, however, led to him being removed from the band later that same year as the band wanted to move forward with their music. Since then he has been involved in a few groups but is currently in the hip hop group Clubs & Spades.

Jamie Cook

Jamie Cook

Jamie Cook joined the band in 2002 when he was 15 turning 16 as one of their guitarists. Since then he has been the second guitarist alongside Alex in the band. He is currently 40 years old.

Nick O'Malley

Nick O'Malley

Nick O’Malley started playing bass when he was 16 years old, however he was not an original member of Arctic Monkeys. He was added to the band in 2006 as a temporary replacement for Andy during touring and the band decided to keep him on as their permanent bassist after that. He is currently 40 years old.
